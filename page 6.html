The 2000s-2024

Ever since the debut of basketball, leagues such as the NBA (National Basketball Association), the CBA (Chinese Basketball Association) and the EuroLeague have all been introduced. And these leagues have all brought incredible athletes such as Kobe Bryant, Shaquille O'Neal, Michael Jordan, LeBron James and so much more from the year 2000. Who could have known that a game that began in a gymnasium could evolve into so much more?
