                              Who we are

Basketball Evolution is a website whose sole purpose is to teach you about the history of basketball. We aim to also educate future generations about the sport and hopefully inspire them to keep the sport alive.

The creator of this website (Aidan Tom Pay Shun) is a student at St. Thomas High School in Pointe-Claire, Quebec. He has been playing basketball for over a year now and has built a love for the sport, and he is always ready to learn more. Aidan also has 1 year experience in HTML coding and is currently learning a new language called CSS.
